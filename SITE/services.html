1. One-on-one Mindful Eating Coaching in-person and virtual counseling available

  • Have you heard the term “mindful eating” or “intuitive eating”, but aren’t sure how this concept might apply in your life?
  • Are you struggling with your relationship with your body and food?
  • Have you tried every diet on the planet only to be discouraged?
  • Are you ready to think less about food and eating, yet feel more connected to your eating experiences more than ever before?

What if it were as simple as listening to your body when it says its had enough food to eat. For many of us we’ve learned to “turn off” our hunger/fullness signals, only to ignore this important biological component which informs us when we’ve had enough nourishment from food. During one-on-one mindful eating coaching, you will learn the fundamental basics of mindful eating concepts and gain lifelong mindful eating tools to incorporate into your daily life. You will get customized hands-on mindful eating practices specific to your eating habits, mindful eating meditation experiences, and one-on-one nutrition coaching support. Most importantly you will have an opportunity to make peace with food and discover a deeper appreciation for the mind body connection.

Disclaimer: I do not counsel patients with eating disorders and a referral to an appropriate medical professional may be provided at any time as deemed necessary. Mindful eating coaching does not substitute for professional psychological care.

2. One-on-one Nutrition Counseling In-person and virtual counseling available

  • Do you struggle with finding appropriate food choices that match your food preferences, or food allergies/intolerances?
  • Have you tried many diets in the past in hopes to lose weight or feel better?
  • Are you wanting to take preventative measures to reduce the risk of developing chronic disease, or are looking to reduce cholesterol, regulate blood sugar, or reduce blood pressure?
  • Do you have a child who is a picky eater and you need support with providing them the right nutrients?
  • Are you looking for ways to eat healthier on a budget?
  • Are you vegan or would like to go more plant-based, but don’t know where to start?

Individualized nutrition counseling is based on personal medical history, food preferences/food allergies/intolerances, and health and wellness aspirations. You will receive individualized nutrition counseling guidance to get you closer to your desired results. If you’re looking to eat healthier on a budget or don’t have much time to cook, Melinda offers simple budget-friendly grocery shopping tips, recipes, and customized meal planning tools for your needs. Have food allergies or intolerances? Melinda provides education on alternative ingredients to keep meals allergy-free and nutritiously balanced. Whether you would like to reach your body’s naturally healthy weight, prevent chronic disease, shift towards a plant-based diet, or find ways to increase the nutrient content in your child’s diet, nutrition counseling with Melinda can support you. Please reach out for a FREE 15 minute consultation see if we would be a good fit.

3. 4 Week Connected Vegan In-Person Group Class -$200

  • Are you looking for a step-by-step class to help you go vegan, or are you already vegan and would like to learn more about vegan nutrition?
  • Are you looking for a vegan support group?
Join us to find out for yourself what it means to be a ‘connected vegan’. In this group class you will get an abundance of vegan nutrition education plus group connection and support. As a group you will have an opportunity to learn from others and share your own stories throughout the weeks. You will learn the fundamental basics of vegan nutrition + lifestyle. Cooking demos, grocery store tour, and recipes will be provided to help get you more comfortable with cooking vegan cuisine. We will discuss simple ingredient swaps when making any recipe vegan-friendly. Debunk common vegan nutrition myths and go over nutrient considerations. Tips for navigating social gatherings and dining out at restaurants. Discuss sneaky non-vegan ingredients commonly found in food products, and how to quickly scan a Nutrition Facts ingredient label to determine if the product is vegan. By the end of this course, you will gain a solid understanding of vegan living and have a toolkit readily available. You will have the opportunity to develop an ongoing connected vegan support group with a class Facebook group page. We will also have a vegan potluck dinner celebration as a group on the last week of class!
