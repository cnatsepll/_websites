Hi I’m Melinda!

Registered Dietitian, Nutritionist, Reiki Master, and ethical vegan.

My views on nutrition and “healthy eating” have evolved over the years and continue to do so.

My lifelong interest in nutrition studies combined with an empathic nature has led me down the path to become a nutrition counselor. Following our inner wisdom allows you to become more attuned to your body's needs . By fostering self-acceptance, determination, and a self-loving embrace, long-lasting change can occur. It is my wish to support others in making long-lasting changes they always wanted to do, but might have needed a nudge in the right direction. I believe our ‘work’ is never done and we are always guided to learn new things. This is why I consider myself to be a teacher, yet an avid learner. There are many lessons to learn from our real life experiences and the stories we’ve told ourselves-whether about our bodies, or what society thinks “health” should look like

I’ve been vegan since 2015 and was vegetarian for several years prior. I love teaching people how easy it is to go vegan, and offering practical solutions to the common obstacles we face. (Read more here about my story for going vegan-blog link) Traditional diet mentality is restrictive and self-limiting. I don’t believe this is how life should be! Finding balance is key to living a more health-conscious life and rids you of unnecessary guilt and unrealistic expectations.

Whether you are wanting to go vegan, or simply move your way to eating more healthful whole foods, I enjoy showing others how living a plant-centered lifestyle doesn’t have to be complicated or time consuming.
