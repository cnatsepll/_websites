Melinda is a Registered Dietitian, Nutritionist and Reiki Master specializing in plant-based nutrition and mindful eating.

As an ethical vegan and veggie lover, her mission is to help others live a more compassionate life by cultivating a nourishing relationship with the mind, body, and spirit.

Whether you're wishing to go more plant-based for health, for your love of animals, environmental concerns, or for all of the above, you’ve come to the right place!

Melinda offers both individualized and group support for developing positive relationships with food, and provide you with the tools to help you reach your personal health and wellness aspirations
