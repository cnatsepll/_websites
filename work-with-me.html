Work With Me

The words “health” and “nutrition” has many different meanings in this day and age. With a quick google search at our fingertips we can find millions of articles on nutrition. It can be quite confusing to know what ‘diet trend’ is real and what’s not.

I like to think that healthy living doesn’t have to be complicated nor does it have one standard definition. In fact, every body is different! I offer clients a multifaceted and individualized view on how to develop an awareness of the foods and eating patterns that align with what your specific body needs, while actually enjoying your eating experience.

For many people, especially those who jump from diet to diet, this is a foreign concept since we’ve been trained to “eat this” “not that” and often associate healthy eating as time consuming and boring.

Health has many different meanings. It’s up to you to decide what that means for you. I believe the key to lifelong health and wellness is to listen to our bodies internal wisdom. With the right tools, you have everything you need to make informed decisions about what you choose to put on your plates.

I specialize in vegan nutrition and intuitive + mindful eating. Whether you are already vegan, or are trying to go more plant-centered, I offer support with making the transition, while taking into account your personal medical history and health and wellness vision.

I offer group classes, and one-on-one consultations. I am available for speaking engagements on plant-based nutrition for all age groups and intuitive + mindful eating. Reach out to me for speaking engagement inquiries.
